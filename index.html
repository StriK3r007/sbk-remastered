Frequently Asked Questions

We offer a diverse range of undergraduate and postgraduate programs across disciplines such as Computer Science, Engineering, Business Administration, Environmental Sciences, Fine Arts, and more. Each program is designed to combine academic rigor with real-world relevance.

You can apply online through our official admissions portal. The application process typically includes filling out the online form, submitting academic transcripts, and appearing for an entrance test or interview (if required). Admission dates and guidelines are updated regularly on our website.

Yes, the university offers a variety of scholarships and financial aid programs based on academic merit, financial need, and special categories such as sports or community service. Details can be found on the Scholarships & Aid section of our website.

Our campus features modern facilities including fully-equipped laboratories, libraries, computer centers, sports complexes, cafeterias, prayer areas, and high-speed internet. We strive to create a learning-friendly and inclusive environment for all students.

Education is the foundation of personal and societal growth, empowering individuals especially women with the knowledge and confidence to shape a better future.
